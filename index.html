The Mind Flayer

The supreme ruler of the Upside Down. A gigantic, spider-like entity composed of silver storms and shadow particles. It controls the Hive Mind, possessing hosts to spread its influence into our dimension.

THREAT LEVEL: EXTREME

Vecna

Formerly Henry Creel (001). The "Five-Star General" of the Mind Flayer. Vecna preys on the trauma of teenagers in Hawkins, killing them in a ritualistic manner to open gates between worlds. "It's time."

TARGETING: HAWKINS

Demogorgon

The soldier. A predatory humanoid creature with a flower-like face lined with needle-sharp teeth. It hunts by blood scent and can tear through the fabric of reality. Tough, fast, and relentless.

STATUS: DANGEROUS

Demodog

An adolescent stage of the Demogorgon. While smaller, they hunt in packs and share a hive mind connection. Fast, agile, and fiercely loyal to the Mind Flayer. "It's a trap."

STATUS: PACK HUNTERS
